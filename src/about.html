ABOUT US

We are a team of dedicated innovators passionate about leveraging technology to support human well-being. PhysioLive was born out of our commitment to blend modern healthcare with real-time data intelligence

Our mission is to assist physiotherapists, caregivers, and patients by providing a smart, sensor-driven dashboard that tracks muscle movement and motion patterns in real time. By combining our skills in embedded systems, data visualization, and software development, we’ve built PhysioLive — a powerful, user-friendly platform designed to monitor physical therapy sessions with precision and clarity.

Whether you're a patient, a clinician tracking patient progress or a researcher exploring motion data, PhysioLive empowers you with actionable insights and automated reporting — all in one interactive tool.
